Gameplay Notes

Special Mechanics

  • She can carry seaplane fighters on Kai.
  • She gains the ability to equip small-caliber guns, daihaitsu and tank on Kai Ni.
  • She gains a special attack on Kai Ni called "Full broadside... Sounds exciting!" (一斉射かッ…胸 が熱いな！) that targets 3 enemies at random.
    • Activation requirements are as follows:
      • Nagato Kai Ni must be the flagship.
      • Another battleship must be in the 2nd position.
      • She must not be more than lightly damaged (小破).
      • The second ship must not be heavily damaged (大破)
      • There must be 6 or more surface ships in the fleet.
        • There must be no submarine in 6-ship fleet. In 7-ship fleet, only 1 submarine allowed.
        • There must be no surface ship in the fleet sunk or retreated via FCF during sortie causing remaining < 6. Ships in escort fleet not affect.
      • Echelon formation needs to be selected in a single fleet OR Cruising Formation 2 for a combined fleet.
    • If triggered, Nagato will do two attacks with a 1.4x post-cap modifier and the other battleship will do a 3rd attack with a 1.2x post-cap modifier.
      • If Mutsu is the 2nd ship, Nagato gains a 1.61x post-cap modifier while Mutsu gains a 1.62x post-cap modifier.
      • If Mutsu Kai Ni is the 2nd ship, both Nagato and Mutsu will gain a 1.68x post-cap modifier.
      • If Nelson is the 2nd ship, Nagato will gain a 1.54x post-cap modifier while Nelson gains a 1.5x post-cap modifier.
      • An AP shell will provide an extra 1.35x multiplicative bonus.
      • A radar with 5 or more LoS stat will provide an extra 1.15x multiplicative bonus.
      • See below for calculated bonus:

    • The attack will consume extra ammo from both participating battleships when triggered. The total ammo consumption will be x1.5 of the original ammo consumption of when the attack is triggered (Source), hence:
    • If it is triggered in day battle, it costs 10% extra ammo.
    • If it is triggered in a night battle node, it costs 5% extra ammo.
    • If it is triggered in a night battle in a normal node, it costs 15% extra ammo.

Trivia

  • She is named after Nagato Provice, modern day Yamaguchi Prefecture.
  • Nagato was part of the orginal game's launch when the game was released in April 23rd, 2013.
  • Nagato was the only surviving Japanese Capital ship after World War II.
  • The "Giant Flash" she speaks of in her sunk and wedding lines is probably a reference to the Able Nuclear Test during Operation Crossroads, where a 23-kiloton air deployed nuclear weapon was detonated over Nagato and 94 other target ships. Nagato had been deliberately placed near the epicenter of both the Able and Baker detonations because the Pearl Harbor attack had been commanded from her bridge.
  • Nagato managed to survive the Able Test unscathed, however she sprung a leak after the underwater detonation during the Baker Test, repair crews were unable to repair the leak due to high radiation levels and she capsized and sank during the night of 29/30 July 1946.
  • The real-life Nagato made a cameo in the animated movie, "The Wind Rises", shown with the serpentine funnel that came with her refitting between 1923-1925.
  • Sunk as a target during Operation Crossroads, 25 July 1946. Currently resting in 110 ft. of water, and upside-down, she is listed as one of the top 10 diving sites in the world.
