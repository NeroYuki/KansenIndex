Description The White Devil. Is supportive of her sister Lexington's aggressive advances on the Commander. Likes crowded places. Always creates "frightening" new recipes using her cooking sense and sudden inspiration.
Voiced by Shiori Sugiura

Saratoga is love. Saratoga is life.
Saratoga is here with a smile to melt your heart.
Saratoga is one of the stronger and easier to use SSR CVs due to her kit and her potential to be run solo.
She can deal AoE damage to all enemies when she sinks an enemy with her (non-special) airstrike. Add the flagship aimed shot to the mix and she has the devastating potential of destroying the whole enemy fleet in one airstrike.
Saratoga gets a special airstrike at night with 100% damage at max level. The highest among special airstrikes albeit unusable in some cases.
All in all a very solid CV, mainly used for solo farming and solo CV comps.

Her downsides? A meh base skill and her night battle airstrike trigger which isn't available in some stages as it disables night battles or starts at night battle, leaving it unusable.
